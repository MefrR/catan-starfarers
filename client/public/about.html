Starfarers — a free online digital Catan: Starfarers

Starfarers is a free, fan-made digital adaptation of the deep-space board game Catan: Starfarers, playable right in your web browser. Voyage into deep space, explore planetary systems, build colonies and spaceports, establish trade stations at alien outposts, survive encounters with merchants and pirates, and race your fleet to 15 victory points. Play solo against AI commanders or online with friends — no download, no purchase.

What is Starfarers?

It's a complete, browser-based version of Catan: Starfarers — the spacefaring spin on Catan where you command a mothership, fly colony and trade ships across the galaxy, and compete to settle new worlds. Everything works online: dice production, trading, building, the flight phase, all 32 encounter cards, the four alien outpost civilizations, and friendship cards.

Features

  • Free, in your browser — desktop, phone, or tablet. Installable as an app (PWA).
  • Single-player vs AI with easy / normal / hard difficulty.
  • Online multiplayer — create or join a room and play with friends.
  • Accounts, friends, and match history when you sign in.
  • Charted or Uncharted (fog) galaxy for extra replayability.
  • Faithful rules: encounters, pirate bases, ice planets, outposts, and friendship cards.

Frequently asked questions

Is there an online version of Catan: Starfarers?

Yes. Starfarers at starfarers.space is a free, fan-made digital version you play in your browser — solo vs AI or online with friends. No download or purchase needed.

Can I play it in the browser, free?

Yes — it runs in any modern browser on desktop, phone, or tablet, completely free, and can be installed as an app.

Can I play with friends online?

Yes. Create or join a room for real-time online multiplayer, or play single-player against AI.

Is this the official Catan: Starfarers?

No — it's an unofficial fan project based on the published rules, not affiliated with or endorsed by Catan GmbH or Catan Studio.

How do you win?

Be the first to reach 15 victory points by building colonies, spaceports, and trade stations across the galaxy.
